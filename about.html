About Me

I am an Architect and Educator passionate about bridging the tangible craft of physical model making with the transformative potential of digital fabrication. My work explores how traditional architectural principles can be enhanced and reimagined through computational design and technologies like 3D printing and laser cutting. I currently channel this focus as the Industrial Engineer Supervisor at UniPod SSD, a makerspace dedicated to fostering innovation.

Background & Education

  • Faculty Member, School of Architecture, University of Juba

Experience and Interests
